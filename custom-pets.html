Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about custom pet orders.

Every pet is sculpted entirely by hand using cold porcelain or high-grade polymer clay. They are finished with detailed acrylic paint and coated with a premium glossy resin glaze to protect them from moisture and dust, ensuring they last forever.

Because there are no molds used and every detail is created from scratch, custom commissions take approximately 3 to 4 weeks. I will share a progress update with you during sculpting so you can check and approve the pose before glazing.

Yes! We pack all custom orders snugly in thick bubble wrap and cardboard boxes, and ship them pan-India with tracking numbers. You'll receive shipping updates as soon as the package is dispatched.

Once sculpting starts, the deposit is non-refundable. Since custom pets are unique pieces built specifically for you, they cannot be returned or refunded once shipped. However, I make sure you approve the product photo beforehand to guarantee you love it!
