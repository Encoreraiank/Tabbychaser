Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about custom orders.

Every charm is handcrafted using handmade cold porcelain clay and carefully sculpted by hand. Each piece is finished with a glossy resin coating for added durability and a vibrant shine. Since every piece is handmade, slight variations make each one unique

Because every piece is handmade, with no molds used, and every detail is sculpted from scratch, custom commissions typically take 4–5 weeks to complete. This allows enough time for sculpting, drying, finishing, and quality checks to ensure your piece is made with care.

Once sculpting starts, the deposit is non-refundable. Since custom creations are unique pieces built specifically for you, they cannot be returned or refunded once shipped. However, I make sure you approve the product photo beforehand to guarantee you love it!
